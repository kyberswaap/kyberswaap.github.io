KyberSwap's protocol utilizes an Automated Market Maker (AMM) model to facilitate trades. The AMM algorithm automatically adjusts the exchange rates based on the supply and demand of the tokens in the liquidity pools, ensuring that trades are executed at fair and transparent prices.
